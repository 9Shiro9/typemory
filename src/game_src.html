Controls

Zombies fall from the sky — just type and the lowest zombie whose word starts with that letter gets targeted. On a phone, use the on-screen keys. No German keyboard needed: for ä ö ü ß just type a o u s (ae oe ue ss work too) · Tab/💡 reveals a letter in Recall (costs points) · Esc/⏸ pause · Mistakes make the zombie faster.
